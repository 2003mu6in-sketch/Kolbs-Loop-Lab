Lab for Your Life

This is a reflection‑driven experiment journal where you pick a problem area in your life (like procrastination, relationships, or self‑doubt), run small experiments, and reflect deeply on what changes. Instead of a linear diary, it focuses on finding patterns over time and showing a “streak”‑style timeline of your repeated attempts to solve real‑life problems. It’s built for people who want to think harder, not just check boxes.
